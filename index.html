About Me

Welcome to my visual journey. I'm Muhammad Uzair, an award-winning photographer and visual storyteller from Sukkur, Sindh, Pakistan.

As a versatile and deadline-driven photographer, I specialize in event coverage, promotional campaigns, and social media content creation. My experience includes leading photography teams at major university events and creating compelling visual assets for admissions campaigns.

I've been recognized with 35 International Photography Awards and maintain an engaged Instagram portfolio @uzairethics_ with 2k+ followers. Currently pursuing Computer Science at Shaikh Ayaz University Shikarpur while developing my photography expertise through platforms like CreativeLive.

My technical skills include DSLR photography, Adobe Lightroom, Photoshop, videography, and video editing with CapCut and Filmora. I'm passionate about capturing authentic moments and creating visual content that resonates with audiences.
